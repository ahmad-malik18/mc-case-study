Three Strategic Bets, Ordered by Leverage
Bet 1
Increase Reach
Scale top of funnel via SEO, paid, instructor social, and cross-selling to MC's 11M+ users.
500K to 750K-1M visitors
Bet 2
Compress Friction
Optimize LP conversion. Eliminate the 12-step Kira gauntlet, duplicate registration, and email handoff.
10% to 12-14% LP / 14-18% video
Bet 3
Re-engage and Convert
Recover abandoned applicants via segmented lifecycle sequences. Convert admitted candidates into paid seats via incentives, installments, and personal outreach.
~1,500 completions + 12-15% admitted-to-paid
Built into Bet 1
Cross-sell to MC's 11M+ users. In-product banners on instructor pages, personalized email, early-access invitations. Zero acquisition cost. High trust.
